This configuration deploys a scalable guestbook application with a frontend and a Redis backend. The frontend is exposed via a NodePort service and is replicated across three pods. The Redis backend consists of a leader pod and two follower pods, with separate services for the leader and followers. The application uses a master-slave replication for Redis, ensuring data consistency and availability. The configuration includes resource requests and limits, as well as liveness and readiness probes to ensure the health and reliability of the application.
